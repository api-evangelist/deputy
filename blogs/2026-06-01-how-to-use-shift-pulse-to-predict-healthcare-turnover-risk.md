Healthcare operations managers face persistent staff shortages, and Deputy's 2025 Shift Pulse Report, based on over 1.5 million post-shift survey responses, found that healthcare workers dominate the bottom of the sentiment scale across all industries. This guide explains how to use Deputy's Shift Pulse tool to identify early warning signs of turnover risk and intervene before losing critical clinical staff.
